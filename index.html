Short Bio

I am currently a senior researcher at Huawei Edinburgh Research Center. I was a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University, co-supervised by Prof. Xudong Liu and Prof. Shuai Ma from 2012 to 2018. Before that, I obtained my BS degree in computer science and technology from Beihang University in 2012. My research focuses on graph querying and graph data management.
